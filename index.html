About Learning Hub

Our Mission

Learning Hub was created to democratize quality education for Class 11 entrance exam preparation. We recognized the challenges students face in accessing comprehensive study materials and expert guidance.

What We Offer

Our platform provides meticulously curated MCQ questions across all subjects, designed by experienced educators to mirror actual exam patterns. Each question comes with detailed solutions and explanations to help students understand concepts thoroughly.

Unique Approach

What sets Learning Hub apart is our adaptive learning system that identifies your strengths and weaknesses, providing personalized practice recommendations to maximize your preparation efficiency.

For Students, By Educators

Founded by Sandesh Panthi, an educator with over 15 years of experience, Learning Hub is built on the principle that every student deserves access to high-quality educational resources regardless of their background or location.
